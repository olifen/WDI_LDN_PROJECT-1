Instructions

2 players, 50 flags. Each player will lay out 4 flags on their side of the board. Then take turns guessing the flag. A correct answer will get you 1 point.

If a flag is guessed correctly, it will be removed from the board. When a flag is removed, a random flag will be put in it's place.

The winner is the first player to 20 points.
